GENRES AND TYPES OF MUSIC

There are many genres in the Music Industry. For example, there is pop, blues,
rock, and rap. Here is a list for more genres in Music:

  • Pop
  • Blues
  • Rock
  • Musical Theatre
  • Jazz
  • Hip Hop
  • Country
  • Funk
  • Soul
  • Folk
  • Singing
  • Orchestra
  • Electronic Dance
  • Popular
  • Punk
  • Reggae
  • Indie
  • Instrumental
  • Rap
  • R&B

Music can also be created in different languages. Mostly, music is known
to be written and listened to in English. But there are songs created
in other languages like Filipino, Korean, Irish, Chinese, Maori and many more.
Sometimes, music can be listened to by itself. It can be produced without voices.
For instance, music can be produced by using instruments like piano,
violin, drums or even using beats.

WHERE TO LISTEN TO MUSIC

You can listen to music by listening on music platforms. You
can listen to songs on streaming music platforms like YouTube,
Spotify, SoundCloud and loads more. Or you could even listen
to music by buying albums or CDs, or even listen by hearing the
radio inside the car!
